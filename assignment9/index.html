AAC For Water Skiing

In water skiing and water sports in general, there are a lot of hand signals and commands that are necessary for communication between the skier and the boat driver. Without being able to speak over the noise of the water and the boat itself, it’s important to know these hand signals. This is not only a challenge for those with disabilities, but also beginners that may not know the hand signals. To fix this problem, we came up with a touch device that would be a direct link between the boat driver and the water skier. With a simple tap of a button, the skier can notify the driver about any common skiing information. Since it’s a water and adventure sport, the device would be waterproof and securely attached to the skier by an arm, leg, or torso strap. It can connect through bluetooth since it is a simple one way system. Making it a two way system isn’t too viable when the skier doesn’t have time to read messages while in the middle of an activity.
